Favorite Movie Streaming Services

Sony's Crackle
Contains a collection of Sony movies/TV shows which are supported by ads. Currently has some of my favorite movies, Resident Evil and Underworld.
Amazon Prime Video
Contains many TV shows and movies that are available with just an Amazon Prime subscription. In addition there exist many channel subscriptions from networks such as HBO,Showtime, and Cinemax that have their own material available for a monthly subscription.

Favorite PC Distribution Services

Valve's Steam
Contains a massive library from AAA to independent games. Steam has sales every 6 months for Summer and Winter.
Good Old Games
Has a good library of games, but not as extensive as Steam. The biggest bonus is that none of the games contain any DRM software restricting the user's ability to use the install file as they wish.

Favorite People in History

Jesus Christ
No person has left a greater impact on my life than when I came to faith in Jesus. He provided stability in my life and hope at a time when I was lost.
Abe Lincoln
Abe Lincoln is one of the most influential historical characters in my life. The challenges he faced, and that he overcame has inspired me to a great deal. He went from having no more than a year of formal education to then becoming one of the United States most influential Presidents.
